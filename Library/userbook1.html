Orbital

Samantha Harvey

Release date: 27 June 2024


Book ID: 8761


Description:

Orbital is a 2024 novel by English writer Samantha Harvey that incorporates elements of science fiction, literary fiction,
and philosophical drama, published by Jonathan Cape in the UK and by Grove Atlantic in the US. It follows six fictional astronauts
over 24 hours on an orbiting space station. Samantha Harvey is an English novelist. She won the 2024 Booker Prize for her novel Orbital,
which drew on conventions from multiple genres and fields, including literary fiction, science fiction, and philosophy.
