Certified Block chain Associate

Blockchain is a digital ledger that records transactions across a network of computers, making it difficult to change, hack, or manipulate the information. It's also known as Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT) Blockchain technology is a structure that stores transactional records, also known as the block, of the public in several databases, known as the “chain,” in a network connected through peer-to-peer nodes.
